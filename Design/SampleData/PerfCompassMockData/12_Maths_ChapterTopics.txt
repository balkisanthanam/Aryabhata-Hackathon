*   **Linear Programming Problem and its Mathematical Formulation:** Formulating real-life optimization problems mathematically.

**Chapter 13: Probability**
*   **Conditional Probability:** Probability of an event occurring given that another event has already occurred.
*   **Multiplication Theorem on Probability:** Calculating the probability of simultaneous occurrence of events.
*   **Independent Events:** Conditions under which the occurrence of one event does not affect another.
*   **Bayes' Theorem:** Finding reverse probabilities based on conditional probability and partitions of sample space.
